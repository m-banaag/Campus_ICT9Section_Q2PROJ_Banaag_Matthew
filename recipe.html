Chilli Cheese Ramen



Chilli Cheese Ramen Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Corn Starch 2 tsp
Cold Water 2 tsp
Milk 1/2 cup
Soy Sauce 1 tsp
Salted Butter 2 tsp
Cheddar Cheese, grated 1/2 cup
Instant Noodles 1 package
Garlic Powder 1 tsp
Cayenne Pepper As needed

Steps to Make Chilli Cheese Ramen

  1. To make the slurry, combine the Corn Starch (2 tsp) with Water (2 tsp) in a small bowl and set aside for later.
  2. Grate the Cheddar Cheese (1/2 cup) and set aside for later.
  3. Add Milk (1/2 cup), Salted Butter (2 tsp), Soy Sauce (1 tsp), Garlic Powder (1 tsp), and Cayenne Pepper (as needed) into a pot, and give it a quick stir. Set the stove to medium-low heat.
  4. Once the butter melts and the milk comes to a low rolling boil (approximately 3-5 minutes), stir the Corn Starch slurry you made earlier and add it to the pot.
  5. Keep stirring until the milk thickens.
  6. Once the milk thickens and starts to bubble, turn off the heat. Add the cheese and stir until it melts and combines well with the milk.
  7. Pour the cheese sauce into your serving bowl and set it aside.
  8. Rinse the pot with hot water to remove the cheese sauce. Refill it with water to cook the Instant Noodles (1 package). Subtract 1 to 1.5 minutes from the noodle cooking instructions for chewier noodles.
  9. Once the noodles are done, drain the water but reserve 1-2 tablespoons for adjusting the cheese sauce's creaminess.
  10. Add the noodles to the cheese sauce in the serving bowl and mix well.
  11. If the cheese sauce is too thick, add 1 tablespoon of reserved noodle water to thin it out. Add more water as needed.
  12. Top with garnishes and enjoy while hot!
