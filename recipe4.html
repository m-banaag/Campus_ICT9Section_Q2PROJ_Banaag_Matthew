Curry Udon



Curry Udon Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Udon Noodles2 servings
Curry Powder1 tbsp
Beef or Chicken (sliced)200g
Onion (sliced)1 medium
Water2 cups
Soy Sauce1 tbsp
Mirin1 tbsp
Potato Starch (optional)1 tsp

Steps to Make Curry Udon

  1. Cook Udon Noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pot, heat a little oil and saut√© the sliced Onion until translucent.
  3. Add the sliced Beef or Chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Pour Water (2 cups) into the pot and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to simmer.
  5. Add Curry Powder (1 tbsp), Soy Sauce (1 tbsp), and Mirin (1 tbsp) to the pot. Stir well.
  6. Simmer for 5-10 minutes to let the flavors meld. Optionally, mix Potato Starch with water to create a slurry and add it to the pot to thicken the curry.
  7. Add the cooked Udon Noodles to the pot and gently mix until coated with curry sauce.
  8. Serve hot in bowls and garnish as desired.
